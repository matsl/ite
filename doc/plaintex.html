Plain TEX

If plain TEX is used, the commands in the previous section have to be replaced by the equivalent commands in this chapter. 

\input ite.tex
The graphics and dvi options are not supported. 
\input trig.tex
Note that `trig.tex' may not be installed on your system. 
\beginiteblock(x_dimen,y_dimen) 
    ...
\enditeblock
\ITEunit=dimension
\beginitebind
    ...
\enditebind
